    • About Me

          BELLA is a R&B/Soul singer-songwriter and actress from New Rochelle, New York. Just 30 minutes outside of New York City. She has been working on her artistic crafts since her freshman year of high school. BELLA is currently a senior at Loyola University of New Orleans where she is studying Popular and Commercial Music. She has performed in numerous venues including: Carnegie Hall, House of Blues, Tipitinas, and multiple well known venues in several countries in Europe. BELLA was also one of the finalists to advance to the 4th round of NBC's "The Voice", right before the broadcasted television audition. BELLA has been a part of numerous plays and was a lead in an Off-Broadway play in New York City. BELLA has been training in acting at The New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts where she studied film & television acting, musical theater, and commercial/voiceover work. BELLA recently wrapped up her first full feature film as a lead character, which will be released later in the Fall. ​​
